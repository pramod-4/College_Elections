Contact Us

Welcome to our College Elections "Contact Us" page! We value your engagement and are here to assist you with any questions, concerns, or feedback you may have. Whether you are a student, faculty member, or a visitor interested in our electoral process, feel free to reach out to us.
You can contact us via email at bmsce.elections@bmsce.ac.in.
If you prefer visiting us in person, our office is located at PJ Block, and our doors are open during our regular office hours.

Additionally, you can use the contact form below to submit your inquiries online.
We are committed to ensuring a transparent and inclusive election process, and your input is valuable to us. Thank you for being a part of our college community, and we look forward to hearing from you!
